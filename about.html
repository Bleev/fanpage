Where did I get the nickname Turbo?

I got the nickname "Turbo" when I first went to this program called Plumas County Restoration and Education in Watersheds (PCREW). It's a month long program where we camp at 8,000 feet and do labor work in the wilderness. While over there, my crew members saw me as energetic and I ran everywhere. Even without a can of Red Bull, I pulled through my work with a positive mental attitude and an outgoing personality!
